About

Are you worried to travel safely in Hongkong because of Covid19?

Our app provides you the customized "Safety Index Score" for Covid19 based on your current location. The mathematical model considers the parameter that calculates this "customized safety index on your location" based on the parameters like number of confirmed cases, number of active cases and number of recovery in the last 14 days in your location

Worried to stand in long queue to visit a pharmaceutical shop during Covid19 in HK?

Our app provides a digital platform for customized medical information. Based on your location you can access the list of all the pharmaceutical shops available within your locality. The details of the different kind of medicines available in each of the shop can be visualized. It enables you check for the availability of your desired medicine without waiting in long queues.
